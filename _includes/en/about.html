Welcome to the next great European PowerShell event!

This conference combines the former “Deutsche PowerShell Konferenz” and “PowerShell Summit EU” into one great big 3-day PowerShell event for Admins and DevOps! Conference languages are English and German. There is one continuous German track and up to three continuous English tracks. With more than 40 international speakers including PowerShell inventor Jeffrey Snover, and more than 60 sessions, this event is one of the most comprehensive leading edge conferences for anyone dealing with PowerShell.
